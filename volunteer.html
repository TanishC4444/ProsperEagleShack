Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about volunteering with Eagle Shack

How many hours can I volunteer each week?

We request a minimum commitment of 2 hours per week, but you can volunteer for as many as you want weekly depending on your availability and our needs. Many volunteers work before/after school and during special events.

Do I need prior experience to volunteer?

No prior experience is required! We provide comprehensive training for all volunteers. Your enthusiasm, reliability, and willingness to learn are the most important qualities we look for.

Can I receive community service hours for volunteering?

Yes! We track all volunteer hours and provide official documentation for community service requirements, scholarship applications, and college admissions. Hours count toward National Honor Society, Key Club, and other service organizations.

How quickly will I hear back after applying?

We typically review applications within 3-5 business days. Selected applicants will be invited for a brief orientation session before their first volunteer shift. Due to high interest, we cannot accept all applicants each semester.
