About Eagle Shack

Learn more about our student-run school store

Our Story

Eagle Shack was established in 2020 as a student-run business initiative at Prosper High School. What began as a small concession stand has grown into a full-service school store providing hundreds of products to students, staff, and visitors.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide convenient access to snacks, beverages, school supplies, and spirit merchandise while creating hands-on business experience for student volunteers. We aim to serve the Prosper High School community with quality products at affordable prices.

Business Education

Eagle Shack serves as a practical learning lab for business and marketing students. Our student volunteers gain real-world experience in retail operations, inventory management, customer service, marketing, and financial management.
